# NVDA’s FY2026 Q3 Beat Confirms Accelerating Momentum; 2025 Price Return Reflects Resilience Despite Sentiment Swings

NVIDIA (NVDA) delivered a clear earnings beat in FY2026 Q3, with EPS and revenue both exceeding consensus, confirming sustained execution strength and demand for Blackwell-class GPUs. We maintain a **bullish stance**: the FY2026 Q3 results reflect not just a one-time beat but accelerating revenue momentum, supported by sequential and year-over-year growth in both net income and single-quarter revenue. Despite negative sentiment from competitive threats and SoftBank’s stake sale, the 2025 price return of +34.8% demonstrates market resilience and continued confidence in NVDA’s structural dominance in AI compute.

NVDA’s FY2026 Q3 EPS of $1.30 surpassed the $1.2746 estimate, generating a $0.0254 (1.99%) positive surprise.
