# NVDA FY2026 Q3 EPS Beat Confirms Accelerating Momentum; Stock Poised for 2025 Outperformance
NVIDIA (NVDA) delivered a positive EPS surprise in FY2026 Q3, confirming sustained earnings momentum and validating continued revenue acceleration, which supports a bullish stance on the stock’s 2025 price return.
NVDA’s FY2026 Q3 EPS came in at $1.30, exceeding the consensus estimate of $1.2746, resulting in a $0.0254 (1.9928%) positive surprise. This marks the third consecutive quarter of EPS beats, following a $0.0219 (2.1301%) surprise in Q2 and a $0.0079 (0.8297%) surprise in Q1. The sequential expansion of the EPS surprise—growing from 0.83% in Q1 to 1.99% in Q3—demonstrates tightening execution and upside to analyst expectations.
